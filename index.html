Market Profitability Analysis: France vs Europe

This project explores financial sales data to identify which factors most influence profit, with a focus on France and a comparison to the broader European market. Key steps include: Data cleaning and preprocessing Exploratory data analysis (EDA) Correlation analysis (France vs Europe) Visualisations using Plotly and Seaborn Business-style presentation of findings The goal is to understand what drives profitability and how patterns differ between regions.
